Assiduity

Assiduity is a mobile app for smart browsing of Maltese legislation.

Assiduity was partly the outcome of a project by the University of Malta funded by the Take-Off Seed Fund Award (TOSFA). Its purpose is to provide easy access to the body of laws in Malta for students and practitioners of the law.

We also acknowledge the financial and legal expertise support from GTG Advocates.
